[orange]Experience the road with Siri![normal] Apple Siri Voice Navigation brings the iconic voices from Apple OS to truck simulator games. With [orange]over 90 voices in 23 languages[normal] to choose from, Siri is here to guide your trucking adventures, making your journey more enjoyable and memorable.

[green]October 11 (4.3)[normal]: This update brings [green]Norwegian Bokmål Siri voices[normal] to the game.


[orange]Introduction[normal]
I am a high school student, and this is my first project of this kind, created for learning and practice.
Thank you for using this mod! Feel free to [orange]share your feedback[normal] or [orange]leave your request[normal]. Your suggestion may be implemented in just a few days!
If you enjoy it, please consider voting or starring it, awarding it with your free Steam Points, or buying me a coffee to support my "work"!
This project is hosted on GitHub at [orange]https://github.com/quinn0823/apple-siri-voice-navigation[normal], where you can contribute to it. You can also view localized descriptions and the latest updates there.

[orange]Compatibility[normal]
[blue]1.43+[normal], compatible with both ETS2 and ATS.

[orange]Usage[normal]
1. Click [orange]Mods[normal] on the title screen to open the mod manager. (You're already here!)
2. Double-click [orange]Apple Siri Voice Navigation[normal] to activate this mod.
3. Open [orange]Options[normal].
4. Go to [orange]Audio - Voice Navigation - Language and voice[normal] and then select a voice you like.
