[b]Experience the road with Siri![/b] Apple Siri Voice Navigation brings the iconic voices in Apple OS to Truck Simulator games. With [b]over 70 voices in 13 languages[/b] to choose from, Siri is here to guide your trucking adventures, making your journey more enjoyable and memorable.

[b]July 03[/b]: 2.0 > [b]3.1[/b]. Regenerate and rebuild 52 out of 56 existing voices, and [b]add 1 language and 20 voices[/b].

[hr][/hr]
[h1]Introduction[/h1]
I'm a 17-year-old student, and this project is my first attempt on creating something like this. It's for learning and practice.

More languages and voices will be added in the future. If the language you speak or voice you prefer aren't available yet, feel free to [b]leave your requests[/b]. Your suggestions might be implemented in just a few days!

Thank you for subscribing! If you like it, please consider leaving a vote, sharing your feedback, using your free Steam Points to give it an award, or [url=https://buymeacoffee.com/jonathanchiu]buy me a coffee[/url] to support my work!

To help improve this project, you can check out the development guide in the [url=https://github.com/quinn0823/apple-siri-voice-navigation]repository[/url] on GitHub. You can also view the descriptions in other languages there.

[h1]Usage[/h1]
[olist]
[*]Click [b]Mods[/b] on the title screen to open the mod manager.
[*]Double-click [b]Apple Siri Voice Navigation[/b] to activate this mod.
[*]Open [b]Options[/b].
[*]Navigate to [b]Audio > Voice Navigation > Language and voice[/b] and select a voice you like.
[/olist]
